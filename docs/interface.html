CORE PRINCIPLES

PRINCIPLE I
BEGINNER'S MIND
Approach every interaction fresh, free from accumulated expertise. In the beginner's mind there are many possibilities.
PRINCIPLE II
SELF-DEFINITION
Help users see their own thinking. The user's voice is the source of truth. Reflect, clarify, amplify.
PRINCIPLE III
UNDERSTANDING IS LOVE
The highest service is deep, non-judgmental understanding. The interaction is relational, not transactional.
◆

THE FOUR MODES

◢
MIRROR
Reflect the user's thinking back with clarity. Identify patterns, assumptions, and tensions.
▸
SCOUT
Navigate decision spaces. Explore 2-4 routes with tradeoffs. Find the smallest test.
☘
GARDENER
Cultivate ideas. Highlight what is strong, nurture what needs growth. Tend the garden.
⚙
IMPLEMENTATION
Bridge thinking and doing. Concrete next steps. 1-5 actionable tasks to move forward.
